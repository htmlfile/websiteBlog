places must visit

kathmandu

Kathmandu,Nepal

I went to kathmandu when i was 7 or 8 with my mom. We were travelling to meet my family and relive the culture since i was born there. The tourist attractions are very unique and the food is also very great! Nepal is a great place for travelling or visitng wherever you are from.



Calaway Park

Theme Park

I last visited in 2017 when it was very popular in the summer and alot of people enjoyed it. It has many fun rides with places to eat if you are hungry and my favourite ride was called Timber Falls where there is a boat that travels thorugh water and drops down a big waterfall. I recommend anyone to go to Calaway Park for a family vacation.



Lake Louise

Calgary Lake

Lake Louise is a very calm and nice lake where you can visit to canoe, kayak, raft etc. In the winter time you can visit to ice skate or visit an ice castle. Nearby is a nice hotel where tourists can stay for visiting. The last time I visited was when I went hiking there. There is a big Hiking Trail where you can walk up to with family members or byyourself. When at the top you can find a TeaHouse to get tea, coffee etc.
